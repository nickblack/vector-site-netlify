## The VECTOR Framework

### **V – Vision**

- **Define a bold, investable direction that aligns your ambition with what customers actually care about.**
    - **Vision**: Define the scale of market leadership your startup could achieve in five years.
    - **Purpose**: What meaningful transformation are we driving that aligns with our motivation and matters to our customers?
    - **Mission**: What measurable milestone will mark success for us in the next 2–3 years and unlock our next phase of growth?

---

### **E – Execution**

- **Build fast feedback loops that turn learning into traction—week by week, without losing focus.**
    - **Weekly Growth Meeting**: A fast feedback loop where your team stays ruthlessly focused on learning.
    - **Quarterly Strategy Review**: Get the helicopter view, challenge your assumptions and make bold decisions without derailing day-to-day growth.

---

### **C – Constraints**

- **Make smart, founder-led trade-offs that respect your runway, time, and investor pressure.**
    - **Funding**: Identify the funding runway needed to hit critical milestones without compromising momentum.
    - **Time**: How long is our runway to execute before we lose optionality?
    - **Stakeholders**: What, if any, expectations might stakeholders have?

---

### **T – Traction**

- **Tried and tested models that pinpoint and double down on what really drives adoption, revenue, and retention.**
    - **Unmet Needs**: What painful, unsolved problem are we addressing that early adopters urgently need resolved?
    - **Growth**: What's the system by which we acquire, activate and retain our users?
    - **Customer Journey**: Map the stages of awareness and engagement to ensure our messaging and product experience match our customers’ mindset.
    - **Four Forces**: Why do our customers switch from their current solution to ours?
    - **Value Prop Canvas**: Clarify the exact value your product delivers to early adopters to sharpen your pitch and roadmap.
    - **Lean Canvas**: A one-page model to stress-test your business assumptions, surface hidden risks early and build alignment on our team.

---

### **O – Operations**

- **Codify what works—from strategy to GTM—so your team can scale with speed and clarity.**
    - **Golden Goal**: The single objective that, if achieved, unlocks your next stage of growth.
    - **OKRs**: Align your team around measurable goals that drive traction and avoid distraction.
    - **Key Drivers**: Distinct customer behaviors that drive your growth, focusing on your one key constraint.
    - **Powerful Positioning**: Articulate the unique value that makes customers choose your product—so they can win more deals, faster.
    - **GTM Manifesto**: Gives total clarity on who you're serving and why they should choose you, so you close the right deals faster, focus your team, and turn every move into traction.
    - **Product Strategy**: A clear, practical roadmap to align your vision, focus your efforts, and make faster, smarter product decisions as a founder.

---

### **R – Refinement**

- **Sharpen your instincts, leadership, and customer insight through deliberate, repeatable habits.**
    - **Customer Interviews**: Learn how to run interviews that surface actionable insights to improve product–market fit.
    - **Growth**: Turn meetings into growth accelerators that drive accountability and insight.
    - **Leadership**: The mindset you need to lead confidently through uncertainty, scale your team, and achieve your startup’s mission.
