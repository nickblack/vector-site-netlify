Vector Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Essence

**Positioning Statement**
*First commercialisation system built for deep tech founders—turning early traction into a repeatable GTM engine without pulling you away from R&D.*

**Core Promise**
Empower technical founders to win first customers, build a scalable sales engine, and get back to innovation.

**Brand Pillars**

* **Founder-First**: Built by founders, for founders.
* **Deep Tech Expertise**: Tailored for complex, high-stakes markets.
* **Tactical & Hands-On**: Strategy plus execution support.
* **Rhythmic Cadence**: Structured weekly and quarterly sprints.

---

## 2. Visual Identity

### Logo & Lockups

* Sits within Nick Black brand: [https://nickblack.us/uploads/nb_onlysymbol_white_aw.png](https://nickblack.us/uploads/nb_onlysymbol_white_aw.png)

- **Primary Logo**: Wordmark “VECTOR” in uppercase, tight tracking, with an arrow motif extending from the V.
- **Secondary Mark**: Stylized V-arrow icon for favicon and social.
- **Clear Space**: Minimum 1× height of the V around all sides.
- **Incorrect Usage**: Do not alter colors, stretch, or rotate.

### Color Palette (Updated)

| Name                | Hex     | Usage                           |
| ------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Vector Purple**   | #BA37F3 | Dominant brand color, hero backgrounds, section accents |
| **Turquoise**       | #1CA6B2 | Secondary action buttons, highlights |
| **Green**           | #148F7C | Primary action buttons (CTAs)   |
| **Primary Dark**    | #272726 | Header, footer, main nav background |
| **Light Gray**      | #F0F0F0 | Section backgrounds, cards      |
| **White**           | #FFFFFF | Backgrounds, negative space     |

**Usage notes:**
- **Vector Purple (#BA37F3)** should be visually dominant, especially in hero sections, gradients, and key backgrounds.
- **Primary Dark (#272726)** is used for the top header, footer, and navigation backgrounds for a modern, high-contrast look.
- **Green (#148F7C)** is the primary action button color (e.g., "Download Playbook").
- **Turquoise (#1CA6B2)** is used for secondary action buttons and accent highlights.
- **Light Gray (#F0F0F0)** and **White (#FFFFFF)** provide contrast and whitespace for clarity and focus.

### Typography

* **Typeface**: Aeonik by CoFoundery (Light, Regular, Bold). Fallback: Inter.
* **Usage**:
  * H1: Aeonik Bold, 64px
  * H2: Aeonik Bold, 48px
  * H3: Aeonik Regular, 30px
  * Body: Aeonik Regular, 16px
  * Caption/Small: Aeonik Light, 14px
* **Hierarchy**: Maintain clear weight contrast; use Light for pull-quotes or less-critical captions.

## 3. Voice & Tone

* **Authoritative**: Speak with confidence in deep tech and GTM.
* **Empathetic**: Understand founder pain points and urgency.
* **Direct & Clear**: No jargon—straightforward language.
* **Energetic & Motivating**: Inspire action and momentum.

**Do** *“With VECTOR, you’ll build your first 5–15 deals in 12 weeks.”*
**Don’t** *“We offer a comprehensive suite of consulting services to streamline go-to-market processes.”*

---

## 4. Key Messaging

### Hero Messaging

* **Headline**: Win your first customers. Build your GTM engine.
* **Subhead**: VECTOR helps deep tech founders turn traction into repeatable sales—so you can focus on innovation.

### Elevator Pitches

* **30-second**: *"VECTOR is the first GTM system built for deep tech founders—combining coaching, tactical models, and embedded support to help you close your first deals and scale revenue without stepping away from R&D."*
* **10-second**: *"Deep tech GTM, simplified: build a repeatable sales engine in weeks, not years."*

---

## 5. Content Guidelines

* **Imagery**: Feature founders in lab environments, code, and hardware scenes. Use purple overlays for hero banners and turquoise accents for support.
* **Icons**: Monoline style; use Slate Gray or Green for consistency.
* **Layouts**: Generous white space; grid-based; leverage purple backgrounds to section content.
* **Quotes & Proof**: Highlight founder testimonials in Aeonik Light italic on Light Gray backgrounds.

---

## 6. Web & Asset Standards

* **Homepage Sections**: Hero with Purple BG; How It Works; Features & Models; Case Studies; Pricing & CTA.
* **Buttons**:
  * Primary: Green fill (#148F7C), white text
  * Secondary: Turquoise outline (#1CA6B2), turquoise text
* **Header/Footer**: Use Primary Dark (#272726) for backgrounds, with white or purple text/icons.
* **Forms**: Minimal fields; field borders in Light Gray; focus states in Purple.
* **SEO Focus**: "deep tech GTM", "commercialisation system", "technical founder sales".

---

## 7. ICP Snapshot for Creative

* **Persona**: PhD-level founder; values precision and speed.
* **Tone**: Insightful, results-driven, no fluff.
* **Visuals**: Crisp, tech-focused; avoid generic stock imagery.
